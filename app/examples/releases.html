0.4.0

Features

  • iceUnit(Tester): can also be used for mocking $resource
  • iceUnit(Tester): skipModuleLoad() available on directive builder
  • iceStorage: new service

Breaking Changes

  • shortened iceUnitTester to iceUnit
  • refactored fluent api of iceUnit builders:
    • iceUnitTester.controllerScopeBuilder(...).build(); becomes iceUnit.builder.controllerScope(...).build();
    • iceUnitTester.serviceBuilder(...).build(); becomes iceUnit.builder.service(...).build();
    • iceUnitTester.directiveBuilder(...).build(); becomes iceUnit.builder.directive(...).build();
  • refactored api to get iceUnit promise mocks
    • iceUnitTester.getPromiseMock(...); becomes iceUnit.mock.promise(...);
    • iceUnitTester.getHttpPromiseMock(...); becomes iceUnit.mock.$httpPromise(...);
